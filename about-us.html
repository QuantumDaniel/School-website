About Telescope international School's

Founded on a commitment to excellence, BrightFuture prepares students academically and morally for leadership and service.

Who we are

Telescope international School's is a co-educational institution founded in 2008. We combine strong academics with practical life skills, character formation, and modern facilities to prepare students for tertiary education and careers.

Our history

Established by educators and community leaders, the school began as a 120-student day school and has grown to a thriving campus with boarding options, modern laboratories and a vibrant extracurricular programme.

Campus & Facilities

Well-equipped labs, ICT centre, library and sports facilities to support holistic learning.
